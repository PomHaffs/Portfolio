All about me

Once upon a time, in June 2016, there was a mathematics teacher whose passion of technology was so great that one day he bravely ran away to complete a GA Web Development Immersive course. He is now hoping to move into the world of Swift and iOS development.


Background

In June 2016, after teaching Mathematics in high schools for six year, I decided it was time to pursue my love for all things tech, and I enrolled on the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly.

With a background in philosophy and mathematics teaching, it is perhaps not surprising, I have found the logic and challenge of coding to be a perfect fit for me. Having lived, breathed and slept code for the past 12 weeks I am now well armed and keen to start writing elegant and efficient development solutions.

Having successfully completed the course I am currently utilising my new development skills in the study of Swift and iOS development. My aim is to gain a position as a Jnr Swift/iOS developer in a firm that will support my continued development.

So if you are looking for a pucky 'young' graduate with plenty of passion and a endless thirst for knowledge let me know.
